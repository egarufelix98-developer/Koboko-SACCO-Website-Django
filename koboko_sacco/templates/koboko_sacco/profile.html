Our Company Profile

Koboko United Savings and Credit Cooperative Society (KUSACCO) is a community based financial institution offering microfinance and other financial services to all its members regardless of their race, sex, creed or marital status. To cater for the growing needs of the growing microfinance clients in Uganda, Koboko United Sacco offers a number of products and services such as savings, credit schemes, agency banking services and tractor services at very competitive rates.

The Sacco’s operations are concentrated within the region of WestNile and in specific koboko District. Its area of operation covers all the sub counties of Dranya, Lobule, Ludara, Midia, Abuku, Kuluba and Koboko Municipality all within the District of Koboko.

Like the old adage ‘’the sky is the limit’’, Koboko United Sacco in the need to serve more people has expanded its operations to cover some parts of Democratic Republic of Congo(DRC) and South Sudan.

OUR MISSION

To provide financial services to its shareholders sustainably so as to improve their standard of living.

OUR MOTTO

Small savings, Big development.

OUR VISSION

To reduce poverty and improve the standard of living of the shareholders.
