    Why Choose Us

    Koboko United Savings and credit Cooperative society limited (Kusacco) was started by a group of women called the koboko United women association KUWA IN 1987 in an era where there were no banking facilities in koboko and yet the people were in dire need of these facilities.

    On the 17th April 2003, Kusacco was registered as a cooperative savings and society with the registration no 6720 under the cooperative act 1991, with a total 0f 243 members. 136 being female, 101 m ales, 06 groups and 03 management staff and has grown from strength to strength as the leading Sacco in koboko with nearly 8,000 members.

    • What is KUSACCO?

      Koboko United Savings and Credit Cooperative Society (KUSACCO) is a community based financial institution offering microfinance and other financial services to all its members regardless of their race, sex, creed or marital status.

    • Where is KUSACCO located?

      KUSACCO is located in within the region of WestNile and in specific koboko District with the Sacco's activities concentrated in these areas. Its area of operation covers all the sub counties of Dranya, Lobule, Ludara, Midia, Abuku, Kuluba and Koboko Municipality all within the District of Koboko.

    • What services does KUSACCO offer?

      Inorder to cater for the growing needs of the growing microfinance clients in Uganda, Koboko United Sacco offers a number of products and services such as savings, credit schemes, agency banking services and tractor services at very competitive rates.
