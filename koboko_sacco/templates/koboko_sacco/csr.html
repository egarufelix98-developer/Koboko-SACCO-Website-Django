Our Cooperate Social Responsibility

At Koboko united Sacco we believe in our customers and a better community in addition to providing financial services.We believe that today’s businesses have to use their strengths to benefit the local community by transforming their lives and livelihoods both socially and economically.

To complement it’s distinguished banking and financial role, Koboko united Sacco makes contributions in cultural, environmental and educational programmes, as well as health incentives.

Some of the projects we have undertaken include, cleaning Hospitals and visiting the sick in Hospitals as part of the Cooperate Social Responsibility (CSR).
